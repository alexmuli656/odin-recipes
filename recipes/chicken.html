Cripsy Fried Chicken Recipe

This crispy fried chicken recipe delivers juicy, flavorful meat with a perfect golden, crunchy coating. Marinated in buttermilk and seasoned with spices, each piece is deep-fried to perfection. Ideal for a comforting meal or party snack, this classic dish is easy to prepare and always a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken wings or drumettes
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

Steps

  1. Place chicken in a bowl with buttermilk. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Mix flour with paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper in a bowl.
  3. Remove chicken from buttermilk and dredge thoroughly in seasoned flour.
  4. Heat 2 inches of vegetable oil in a deep skillet or fryer to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Fry chicken in batches for 8–10 minutes, turning occasionally, until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Drain on paper towels or a wire rack. Serve hot.
