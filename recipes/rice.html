Fluffy White Rice

This is a basic recipe for cooking fluffy white rice on the stovetop. It serves as a great side dish for many meals.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil (optional)

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a medium pot, combine rice, water, salt, and butter or oil.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium-high heat.
  4. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and cover the pot with a lid.
  5. Simmer for 18â€“20 minutes, or until the water is fully absorbed.
  6. Remove from heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes.
  7. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve.
