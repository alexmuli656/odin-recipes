Fried Cabbage Recipe

Fried cabbage is a quick and delicious dish made by sautéing chopped cabbage with onions and spices. It’s a flavorful and healthy side that pairs well with rice, ugali, or meat stews.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium head of cabbage, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tomato, chopped (optional)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 stock cube (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  2. Add sliced onions (and garlic if using) and sauté until soft and slightly golden.
  3. If using tomatoes, add them now and cook until soft.
  4. Stir in the chopped cabbage. Cook while stirring occasionally.
  5. Add salt, black pepper, and stock cube. Mix well.
  6. Continue cooking for about 10 minutes or until the cabbage is tender but not mushy.
  7. Remove from heat and serve warm as a side dish.
