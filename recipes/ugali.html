Tasty Ugali Recipe

Here’s an appropriately sized recipe description for making Ugali, a popular East African staple food, typically made with maize flour and served with vegetables, stews, or sauces:

Ingredients

  • 2 cups maize (corn) flour
  • 4 cups water
  • A pinch of salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Pour 4 cups of water into a medium-sized saucepan and bring to a boil.
  2. Slowly add maize flour to the boiling water while stirring continuously to prevent lumps.
  3. Continue stirring and pressing the mixture against the sides of the pan until it thickens and starts pulling away from the sides (about 10–15 minutes).
  4. Cover and let it steam on low heat for 3–5 minutes.
  5. Mold the ugali into a dome shape using a spoon or wet bowl. Serve hot with vegetables, sauce, or stew.
